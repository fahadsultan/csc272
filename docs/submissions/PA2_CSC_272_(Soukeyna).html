‚ÄúWhat‚Äôs in a name?‚Äù

The Social Security Administration (SSA) publishes annual data on the most popular baby names, with records dating back 143 years to 1880.

You can learn more about this dataset on the SSA website. For this assignment, you don‚Äôt need to download any files to your computer.

To access the data for a particular year, simply use the following URL structure:
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fahadsultan/csc272/main/data/names/ followed by yob<YEAR>.txt, where <YEAR> is the desired four-digit year.

For example, the 2022 data is available at:
https://raw.githubusercontent.com/fahadsultan/csc272/main/data/names/yob2022.txt.

You can replace ‚Äú2022‚Äù with any year between 1880 and 2022 to access the corresponding data, such as:
- yob1880.txt for 1880
- yob2021.txt for 2021
